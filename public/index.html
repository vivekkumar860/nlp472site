Lab Practicals & Experiments

Ten hands-on labs mapped to the theory units.

  1. 1

    Text Preprocessing & Vectorization

    Unit I

    Build a preprocessing pipeline (tokenization, lowercasing, noise removal, stopword removal, stemming/lemmatization) and convert the processed text into numerical representations using Bag-of-Words and TF-IDF.

  2. 2

    Embedding Visualization with PCA / t-SNE

    Unit II

    Load pretrained Word2Vec or GloVe embeddings, select a set of words, compute their embeddings, and visualize relationships in 2-D space using PCA or t-SNE.

  3. 3

    CBOW vs. Skip-Gram

    Unit II

    Train CBOW and Skip-Gram Word2Vec models on a sample text corpus, compute word similarities and analogies, and compare the performance of both models.

  4. 4

    Semantic Cluster Visualization

    Unit II

    Load pretrained GloVe or Word2Vec embeddings, select word groups from different semantic categories, reduce dimensionality with PCA or t-SNE, and visualize the embeddings to interpret clusters and relationships.

  5. 5

    LSTM Sentiment Analysis

    Unit III

    Build a sentiment analysis model using an LSTM network on a dataset such as IMDB movie reviews or SMS Spam. Compare its performance with a Bag-of-Words or TF-IDF baseline.

  6. 6

    Bidirectional LSTM for NER

    Unit III

    Implement a Bidirectional LSTM model for Named Entity Recognition using the CoNLL-2003 dataset or a smaller annotated set, labelling words as Person, Location, Organization, or Other.

  7. 7

    Seq2Seq Translation with Attention

    Unit IV

    Implement a sequence-to-sequence model with Bahdanau or Luong attention to translate short English sentences into another language using a small parallel dataset. Compare quality with and without attention.

  8. 8

    Attention Summarization (ROUGE)

    Unit IV

    Build an attention-based sequence-to-sequence model to generate short summaries from news headlines or paragraph datasets. Train briefly on a subset and evaluate generated summaries using ROUGE scores.

  9. 9

    Fine-tune BERT for Classification

    Unit V

    Fine-tune a pretrained BERT model for sentiment classification on IMDB reviews or SMS Spam. Evaluate with accuracy and F1-score, and analyze how BERT's contextual embeddings improve classification.

  10. 10

    GPT-2 Text Generation

    Unit VI

    Use a pretrained GPT-2 model to generate text from a given prompt. Apply multiple decoding strategies and compare the fluency, creativity, and coherence of the generated outputs.
